ABOUT US

Jesus' will for humans is rooted in love, compassion, and the pursuit of righteousness. Throughout His life, He taught that humans should love one another as He has loved them, emphasizing forgiveness, kindness, and humility. Jesus encouraged people to seek the Kingdom of God first, living in a way that reflects His teachings and values. He called for individuals to live with a servant's heart, caring for the poor, the sick, and the marginalized, and to seek peace in their relationships. Above all, Jesus desired for humanity to have a personal relationship with God, embracing faith, repentance, and grace. His will is not just about following laws but living with an open heart, showing mercy, and striving to become more like Him in every aspect of life.
